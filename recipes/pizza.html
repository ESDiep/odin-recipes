Pizza

Description

Pizza is a very popular Italian dish.

Ingredients:

  • Flour
  • Tomato sauce
  • Cheese
  • Meat of your choice
  • Basil

Steps:

  1. Make the dough
  2. Press and flatten the dough into a circular shape
  3. Spread the tomato sauce into a thin layer on the dough
  4. Add cheese, then meat and baisl on the top
  5. Bake the pizza
