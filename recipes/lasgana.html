Lasgana

Description:

Lasgana is a versatile pasta dish that is easy to make with the following steps:

Ingredients:

  • Meat
  • Tomato
  • Spcies
  • Lasgana noodles
  • Cheese
  • Eggs

Steps:

  1. Make the meat sauce.
  2. Cook the noodles.
  3. Make the ricotta mixture.
  4. Layer the lasagna
  5. Cover with foil and bake
  6. Let the lasagna rest before serving
